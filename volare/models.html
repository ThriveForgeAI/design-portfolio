Technical sheet

Aurelio V12 — The pure one.

Six and a half litres of naturally aspirated obsession, wrapped in a monocoque of forged carbon and tuned to sing to nine-and-a-half thousand rpm. The Aurelio is the purest expression of Volare's refusal to compromise.

Every Aurelio is hand-built in Sant'Agata, with a delivery time of approximately fourteen months from configuration.

Tempesta — The daily supercar.

The super SUV rewritten from first principles. Four doors, four seats, four-wheel-drive — and a powertrain borrowed from the Corsa GT3. The Tempesta is how a supercar makes it through a Sant'Agata winter without apologising.

Corsa GT3 — The grid car.

The racing car at the root of everything Volare makes. A naturally aspirated V10 tuned to FIA regulations, a sequential gearbox shared with Formula machinery, and a body homologated for every major GT3 series on the planet.
