Questions we hear often

Is Tessera really free?

Yes. The runtime, the CLI, and the HTTP server are Apache 2.0 and free forever. Paid tiers add convenience layers on top — cloud sync, team sharing, SSO — they don't gate core functionality.

Do you send my prompts anywhere?

Never. Models run entirely on your hardware. Even on paid tiers, cloud sync is opt-in and end-to-end encrypted. Community tier has zero outbound calls.

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Yes. Prorated in both directions. Cancel anytime; no retention calls, no sales gatekeeping.

Does Enterprise include a custom deployment?

Air-gapped deployments and VPC setups are available on Enterprise. Talk to sales about requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cards (Stripe) for Pro. Cards or annual invoicing (wire / ACH) for Enterprise.
